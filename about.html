About Dijo Tsa Setso Shop On-line

What we are about

The "Dijo tsa setso" is a small company that was established in 2021 at Maraton Informal Settlement,Germiston 1401 by Funanai Mbendzi and Nelisiwe Mbendzi. The food is delicious and well prepaid. We sell traditonal food from different tribes, like papa and mogodu; rice and chicken ; papa and beef stew.

Nelisiwe Mbendzi: CEO

Nelisiwe Mbendzi is a 23 year old individual who was born in krugersdrop,Swaneville ext 5. She loves to cook and reading new recipes.

Funanai Mbendzi: CO-Founder

Funanani Mbendzi is a 31 year old individual who was born in Folobodwe, Musina. She is a co-founder of our small company,she loves cooking and cleaning.
